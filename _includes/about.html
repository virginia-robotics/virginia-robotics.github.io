About

The Virginia Robotics Symposium (VRS) brings together robotics researchers from across the state of Virginia. VRS is a one-day event with research talks, poster presentations, and social activities. The goal of VRS is to build connections, disseminate research ideas, and facilitate the creation of larger, multi-institutional, and interdisciplinary teams.
